About

I am a recent MSc graduate in Biomedical Engineering, specialising in Medical Data Analysis, from the University of Oulu, Finland. My background combines electrical and electronic engineering with a master's degree focused on quantitative and computational approaches to medical imaging.

My work centres on deep learning methods for MRI segmentation and analysis, conducted at the Center for Machine Vision and Signal Analysis (CMVS). I am currently seeking PhD and research positions in medical image analysis, computational imaging, and applied deep learning.
